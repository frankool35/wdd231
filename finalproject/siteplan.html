Lagos Tourism Guide – Site Plan

Site Name

Lagos Tourism Guide

This name was selected because it clearly describes a website focused on helping visitors explore major attractions and activities in Lagos.

Site Purpose

The Lagos Tourism Guide website provides information about tourist attractions, cultural spots, food destinations, and travel tips for anyone planning to visit Lagos.

Scenarios (User Questions)

  • “Where can I find the best tourist attractions in Lagos?”
  • “Which places in Lagos are safe and affordable to visit?”
  • “How do I contact tourist information centers?”

Color Scheme

Primary Color – Deep Navy (#003366)

Used for: headings, navigation bar, buttons.

Accent Color – Warm Gold (#FDB813)

Used for: highlights, icons, call-to-action sections.

Typography

Poppins – headings

Roboto – body text
